THE LOST TREASURE

Mystery of the Ancient Temple
🔊 Audio synthesized in-browser · Use WASD / Arrows to move · E to interact

How to Play

Move with WASD or Arrow Keys. Press E to interact with chests, signs, levers and puzzle objects. Open your Inventory (I) to review collected items and clues. Explore each area fully — every puzzle's solution is hidden somewhere in the environment. Avoid traps and hazards; they cost you health. Progress through five regions to reach the legendary treasure. Press Esc or the pause button to pause and save.

Credits

A browser adventure prototype — built with HTML5 Canvas and vanilla JavaScript. All visuals are procedurally drawn; all sound is synthesized live with the Web Audio API. No external assets required.
